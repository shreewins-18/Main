Agile Methodology:

Agile means “incremental, allowing teams to develop projects in small increments".
Examples of Agile methodology frameworks: Kanban, Extreme Programming
Scrum is one of the many types of agile methodology,
known for breaking projects down into sizable chunks called “sprints.”
